Chocolate Cake recipe

A simple and practical recipe for the most beloved Brazilian cake.

Ingredients

Batter: 4 tablespoons of cocoa powder, 2 tablespoons of butter, 3 cups of wheat flour, 4 eggs, 2 tablespoons of baking powder, 1 cup of milk, and 3 tablespoons of sugar.

Instructions

1 - Blend the eggs, cocoa powder, butter, wheat flour, sugar, and milk in a blender for 5 minutes.

2 - Add the baking powder and gently mix it with a spatula.

3 - Pour the batter into a greased baking pan and bake in a preheated medium oven (180 ºC / 356 ºF) for about 40 minutes. Make sure to use a tall baking pan for this recipe: since it contains two tablespoons of baking powder, the batter rises significantly
